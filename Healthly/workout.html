Work out plans for a beginner

Working out is a core part of a healthy lifestyle.

Without the nutrients and right diet losing weight can be challenging.

Here are some workout plans to help you get started:

Day Activity Level Description
Monday Beginner Cardio 30 minutes of cardio
Tuesday Rest/Light Activity Rest up, but some light activity is recommended
Wednesday Full-Body Strength Training Full-Body Strength Training
Thursday Active Rest Active rest
Friday Full-Body Strength Training Full-Body Strength Training
Saturday Light Cardio/Mobility Light Cardio or Mobility
Sunday Full Rest Day Full day of rest you earned it

These aren't for everyone so here are some exercises you can do at home:

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Squats: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Plank: Hold for 30-60 seconds, 3 times
  • Jumping jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Remember even just running 30 minutes a day can help alot.
